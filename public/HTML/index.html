!----Our Features----!

Visit below to know more about our features

KisanToMiddlemen

Farmers generally face issues with selling of their crops after harvesting.They don't know about whom should they sell to increase their profits, KTM is a one-on-one interaction between farmer and middlemen in their respective location.

Crop Diagnoise

This feature enable farmers to detect crop diseases at early stages of the farming cycle,some diseases if not detected earlier can damage the entire crop.Farmers can upload the images of their crop,our ML model detects the diseases is there or not and also if it is present then what kind od disease is present.

Schemes

It contains schemes for farmers.Many of the farmers lack knowledge about the schemes and unable to get the benefits from them. This will help them increase their knowledge about the schemes of both central and state.

Modern farming

Still many farmers are cultivating in their ancient methods,due to which they are unable to maximize their profits .This feature has videos of modern farming methods using tractor and many machinaries to reduce crop loss.methods like sprinkling system,drip irrigation can use based on water availability

Community

It is farmers community in which all farmers can join. farmers can post questions related to their crops as well as can answer questions of other farmers.it will help to increase their knowledge.

Location

It has a location based feature in which it asked to give access to detect their location, after which farmer can avail services like weather,soil type ,Air Quality Index etc.
